About This Event
Dillon's Western Trails Arena hosts a 4D Barrel Race on October 17, 2026 at 37839 W Grand in Morristown, Arizona, produced by SSR Productions. The flyer lists divisions as Youth, Middles, Senior, Futurity, and Derby. Time-only runs are available from 5:00 to 6:30 PM, with open competition starting at 7:00 PM. Entries are accepted on SaddleBook. This event is part of a Buckle Series requiring attendance at a minimum of four events, with buckles sponsored by Mountain Country Equipment. Contact Hailey at (435) 681-0058 or visit ssrproductions.org for more information.
