About This Event
The Desert Freedom Classic Run for the Brave is a Memorial Weekend barrel race scheduled for May 22 and 23, 2026 at Horseshoe Park and Equestrian Centre in Queen Creek, Arizona, produced by B.R. Diamond Spur Barrel Production. The flyer lists $1,000 added money with prizes including buckles, a custom tack set, and random drawn prizes. Divisions are Open 5D at $50 entry, Youth 3D at $35, Rookie 3D at $20, and Peewee at $10, with an arena fee of $15 per horse per run. Friday exhibitions run from 4:30 to 6:00 PM with the race starting at 6:30 PM. Saturday exhibitions run from 7:30 to 9:00 AM with the race starting at 9:30 AM. Unlimited exhibitions are listed at $10 per horse. Weekend average buckles are available for Open, Youth, Rookie, and Peewee divisions. The outdoor Tuff Shed Arena will be used. Stalls and RV hook-ups are listed as available. A dress code is noted. Contact by text at 4807558809 or 6024324382, or by email at RaceBRDS@gmail.com.
