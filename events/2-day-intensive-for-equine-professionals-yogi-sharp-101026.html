About This Event
The 2 Day Intensive for Equine Professionals with Yogi Sharp of The Equine Documentalist is scheduled for October 10 and 11, 2026 at Jackpot Veterinary Center, 6745 N La Cañada Dr, Tucson, AZ 85704. The flyer lists the focus as Equine Biomechanics and Myofascial Connections Through Hoof Function, with lectures on farriery science and practical demonstrations included over both days. Lunch is provided on both days. Tuition is listed as $400, increasing to $500 after July 1, 2026. The flyer notes limited spots are available and encourages early registration. Contact by call or text at 520-461-0467 for more information and to register.
